ABOUT US

MTGraphy is an international quality photography company founded in 2004. Our service is to provide premium quality photos for both corporate and personal needs. We can make photos for company profile or corporate needs, advertising, product, editorial and for other marketing needs. Our personal & creative approach has earned us a trusted reputation for producing premium photos and is supported by our well-trained customer service.

Based in Malang, Surabaya and Jakarta, we can provide photo shoots in our studio or at any location throughout the country or abroad.

The clients we have served are Metro TV, Jawa Pos, Trans 7, PT. PJB (PLN), PT. Hotel Indonesia, Natour, BPMIGAS, Ministry of Women's Empowerment and Child Protection, Eijkman Institute, BRI, Bank Syariah Mandiri, BNI 1946, BNI Syariah, Royal Senyiur Hotel, Balimuda United, Lenovo, Taman Safari Indonesia, Universitas Brawijaya, Larissa Aesthetic Center, Ministry Finance, Syaiful Anwar Hospital Malang etc.

The existence of your company is very important to us, therefore we strive to understand the needs of our clients in order to achieve the desired results. We will listen and provide you with the best service.

At MTGraphy we have a team that is highly skilled and passionate about their work. You will have an amazing experience with our friendly photographers and their production team. They will make sure all your photography needs will be met with all their advantages.
